About Shalini

Born in Mauritius with roots that intertwine the best of Mauritian and Indian traditions, Shalini cooks the way families do—slow, careful, and generous. Every dish is a love letter to home: fluffy rotis, vibrant curries, street-style snacks, and sweet finishes inspired by island markets and Indian festivals.

Shalini’s Corner is part-time and small-batch: when the pots are empty, that’s it! Follow the weekly menu, order ahead, and treat yourself to flavors you won’t forget.
