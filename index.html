FAQ

Do I need special equipment?

A pressure cooker or Instant Pot is needed. A standard stove, a pan/pot, and basic utensils are perfect.

What if I can’t find certain spices?

We provide smart substitutions and brand tips so you always have a path to success.

Are sessions recorded?

Yes. You’ll receive a video recap and PDF recipe after each class.

What if I miss a live session?

You still receive the recap video and recipe. Pro/Private can request notes for tricky steps.

Dietary needs?

We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten‑conscious swaps in most recipes. Private plans can fully customize menus.
